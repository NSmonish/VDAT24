28th International Symposium on VLSI Design and Test (VDAT-2024)
1st September - 3rd September

CALL FOR SPONSORS

Sponsorship opportunities

  • It gives us immense pleasure to present you with an opportunity to contribute and participate as one of the sponsors of the 28th International Symposium on VLSI Design and Test (VDAT-2024) to be held in an offline mode at Vellore Institute of Technology, Vellore during 01-03 September 2024. The conference will provide a platform for academicians, researchers, and engineers who are actively engaged in the field of VLSI design to share their experiences, research results, and innovative ideas and discuss the practical challenges encountered and the solutions adopted.
